aMahanna

SWE & Tech Lead at ArangoDB

2000 - 2023: I grew up in Ottawa, Ontario, where I graduated from the University of Ottawa with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science. As part of the Winter 2023 Honours batch, I wrote & presented Semantics in Graph Databases: Extending ArangoDB's support for RDF, Ontologies, and beyond (link). The work was awarded the Cognos Prize, and I continue to maintain the project on Github.

2021: I first discovered ArangoDB while applying for Shopify's internship program of Summer 2021. The Shopify Developer Challenge for that semester was to build an image repository to perform any kind of CRUD on images. I submitted my project, and then failed Shopify's "life story" interview. So I took the project to ArangoDB, presented it, and was hired as an intern in Fall 2021.

2024 - Present: I moved to Brooklyn, New York, and continue to work at ArangoDB, building software for Graph Analytics, Graph Machine Learning, and GraphRAG: docs.arango.ai/ai-suite. I also lead a team of 5 junior engineers and am responsible for ArangoDB's technical partnership with NVIDIA, where I maintain badged access. Through this partnership, NVIDIA adopted ArangoDB internally: arango.ai/case-studies/nvidia-nvbugs.
